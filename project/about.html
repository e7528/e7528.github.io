What is Pokémon GO?

Pokémon GO is a mobile game developed by Niantic in partnership with Nintendo and The Pokémon Company. It uses augmented reality (AR) and location-based services to bring the world of Pokémon to life. Players can explore real-world locations to catch Pokémon, join battles, complete quests, and team up with other Trainers. Since its release in 2016, it has become one of the most popular AR games in the world.
